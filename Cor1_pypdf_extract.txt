Battery Life
Requirement HWRS-480: The battery shall have at least 75% of its initial capacity 
after 400 charging cycles.
-
Battery datasheet indicates "Cycle Life" >= 500 cycles-
Battery test specification indicates:-
ChatGPT estimates these endpoints to be around ~95% to ~5-10% capacity○
This data is for one cell. Our pack is 2 cells in series, 3 in parallel○
Our idle current draw is ~1A at level 5 brightness. Our default brightness will be 
Level 4 (800 mA). For a 10.2Ah battery, this gives us 12.75 hours
-
DMM current measurements across power modes:
In ship mode, the internal leakage current is reduce. The internal TI battery 
controller is placed into sleep mode.
-
Measured current from the battery to the main board while in ship mode
A DMM shows current ramps slowly from ~1 to ~45uA over a minute period○
-
Plugging DC in while in ship mode and device turned off causes current from battery 
to jump to ~300uA. After about 5 seconds the current jumps to 48 mA in the 
opposite direction (battery is charging).
-
Removing DC without turning the unit on leaves the battery in ship mode.-
Turning unit on does not change the 48 mA going into the battery-
Battery % per the home screen icon is about 50-60%-
Removing DC while unit is still on causes ~880mA to flow from the battery into the 
board
-
Turning the device off results in the same behavior as when the device was in ship 
mode (ramp from 1 to 45uA)
